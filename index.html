We put the focus on your students

Put the focus of your administrative software back where it should be - on your students. All the actions in our software revolve around the student's account which greatly simplifies navigating around the application. We call it Student Centered Design.

  • Multiple contacts to handle all family situations
  • View billing history and current billing status
  • Summary of private lesson notes and any parent emails
  • Lesson attendance and make-up lesson scheduling
  • Family accounts let you link siblings for easier billing
  • Custom charge per student gives you billing flexibility

Great parent communication = referrals

Good communication with your student's parents is incredibly important. It improves your referrals, minimizes scheduling mixups, and improves billing collections. Trillo makes communication a central part of your daily routine.

Trillo lets you....

  • quickly send parents a note at the end of a lesson. Reminders on what to work on, how the lesson went, etc.
  • control which contacts the note is sent to.
  • record a separate private note about each lesson for yourself.

  • Use the iPad camera to replace the student icons with their picture *Coming Soon*

Why web based software?

Wait - what exactly is web based software?

Web based means the software doesn't get installed on your computer. You access it through a regular web browser like Internet Explorer or Firefox. There are a LOT of advantages to web based software, many of which we list on the right. Feel free to contact us if you have more questions about how Trillo works.

  • Access Trillo from multiple devices - all you need is a web browser and Internet access!
  • The software is always up-to-date. No more software updates or patches. We take care of that for you.
  • Your data is safe and backed up. We make sure your data is secure so you don't have to worry about it.
  • No 'IT guy' needed - all you need is a web browser.
