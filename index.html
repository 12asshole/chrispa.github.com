Do you teach classes for children?

Trillo enables online registration & payment for your customers, and ongoing student managment for your business. You teach, we'll handle the rest.

Sign up below to be notified when Trillo is live.

Simple Online Registration

Beautiful,simple, well-thought-out registration forms that your clients can use to register for your classes. Capture ALL the information needed - emergency contact information, physician information, allergy, special needs, etc. Or don't - it's your choice to capture only what you need.

We allow (but don't require!) your customers to create an account, so they don't need to enter all their information every time. Improve the percentage of customers taking repeat classes by making it super easy to sign up for their next class.

Ongoing Student & Class Management

You didn't start teaching classes so you could always be doing data entry. Trillo is a class and student management application - no more form submissions ending up in your email inbox. Gain better insight into your customers and bring a new level of power to your email marketing. And easily export your data out of Trillo whenever you need it.

  • Manage your classes by session, instructor, location. Easily filter which classes show on what registration form.
  • Collect different levels of information for different students (children vs adults.)
  • Class registration changes from open to full when the class is full. No more emailing/refunding when a class is oversubscribed!
  • Trillo is web based - no software to install, no updates or patches. Access from any device with a browser (it works great on iPads!)

No Fee Online Payment

Stop paying merchant fees, Paypal™ Business Account fees, or frankly any monthly fees to accept credit cards. We built support for Stripe into Trillo - it's the easiest way to allow online payment. You can learn more at https://stripe.com but here is what is important: NO fees unless you charge a card. No setup fees, no monthly fees, no card storage fees, no hidden costs: you only get charged when you earn money.

A virtual terminal built in to Trillo allows you to accept cards over the phone or in person. Stripe integration is free - we charge you nothing to add online payments. Prefer to use something else? No problem.
