Services That Power Trillo

This is a list of some of the world class services we use in order to provide Trillo to our customers and to people signing up for their classes. We highly recommend all of them!

Heroku - Cloud Application Platform

Heroku is the leading cloud appliaction platform. They host our application and keep it up and running.

Github - Code Hosting Service

This is where we store and back-up our code. Github has over 2.8 million customers. Love the logo!

Mandrill - Transactional Email

Mandrill is an email service by the creators of Mailchimp, one of the leader email marketing platforms. Mandrill makes sure your registration confirmation emails get sent.

Honeybadger - Error Management Service

This services helps us be super responsive to any errors that happen within Trillo.

Stripe - Simple Online Payment Acceptance

Stripe makes it extrememly easy and cost-effective to start accepting credit cards on the web.
