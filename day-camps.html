Simple Online Registration

Beautiful, simple, well-thought-out registration forms that your clients can use to register for your classes. Capture ALL the information needed - emergency contact information, physician information, allergy, special needs, etc. Or don't - it's your choice to capture only what you need.

Beautiful registration forms give your customers confidence when signing up online. By making it super easy to sign up for their next class we improve the percentage of customers taking repeat classes.

Ongoing Student Management

You didn't start teaching classes so you could always be doing data entry. Trillo is a class and student management application - no more form submissions ending up in your email inbox. Gain better insight into your customers and bring a new level of power to your email marketing. And easily export your data out of Trillo whenever you need it.

Child / Sibling / Parent accounts are linked, giving you better visibility into the 'Family' as your customer. Highlight the important details your staff needs like medical needs, peanut allergies, and behavioral issues.

Flexible Ways To Charge

Trillo allows you to charge however you'd like, and keep track of who still owes you money. You can accept registrations with no charge, charge the full amount of the class, or charge a deposit (fixed fee or a %.) Students are listed as Paid or Balance/Partial Balance Due so the instructor knows who to expect checks from.

A Simple Way To Accept Credit Cards

If you are looking to accept credit cards, Trillo integrates with Stripe, a great new option without all the traditional monthly fees and long-term contracts. Accepting credit cards can significantly improve your cash flow, and help reduce your cancellation rate.

Payments can be a tricky area - feel free to contact us to discuss what might work best for you. Stripe is available today in the US and Canada - more International options are coming soon!
